New York Cheesecake Recipe

Ingredients

  • Graham crackers
  • Butter
  • Cream cheese
  • White sugar
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • All purpose flour
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Pre hear oven to 350 degrees
  2. Crush Graham crackers and add melted butter
  3. Make the crust
  4. Mix the cream cheese and sugar together.
