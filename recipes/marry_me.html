Merry Me Chicken Recipe

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • flour, salt and pepper
  • Olive oil and butter
  • Garlic
  • Chicken or vegetable stock
  • Heavy cream
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Chili flakes, oregano, basil and thyme

Steps

  1. Flour the chicken in seasoned flour
  2. Oil a pan on medium heat and add butter
  3. Saute garlic
  4. Season with salt and black pepper.
