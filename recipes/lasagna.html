Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasoning
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Chesses, such as parmesan, mozzarella and ricotta
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the sauce
  2. cook the noodles
  3. Layer the lasagna
  4. cover with foil and bake
  5. let lasagna rest
